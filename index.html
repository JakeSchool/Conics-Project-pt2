1. Circle

    A circle is created when a plane is perpendicular to the vertical axis. (Red)

  2. Elipse

    An Elipse is formed when the angle formed by the plane is greater to the vertex angle. (Green)

  3. Parabola

    A parabola is formed when the angle formed by the plane is equal to the vertex angle, intersecting one cone. (Blue)

  4. Hyperbola

    A hyperbola is formed when the angle formed by the plane is less than the vertex angle, intersecting both cones. (Dark Gray)
